# Give God 56 Seconds.. Don't Skip This Message.

**Canal:** For Purpose Driven Men
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VcVTOR7zXrM

---

Your current season is a test. It's not your destination. And a lot of people, they get caught up in how they feel. They get caught up in the moment. They feel like quitting. They don't want to keep going on with life, and they don't want to keep going on with the season that they're in. But I'm here to tell you, and God put this message on my heart for you, bro, to keep going because right now you are just in a season, and seasons change. There's winter, there's fall, but there's also spring and summer, my friend. You have to keep going because sometimes we're in a season so we can learn the lessons that we need to learn so we can be better prepared for our blessings when it's here. Because it's not just about getting the blessings that we're praying for. It's also about maintaining them and keeping them and multiplying them in order to stay successful, in order to keep the blessings. So don't quit. Keep going and keep building that grit. Keep building that discipline. I believe in you. God bless you, and thank you for watching.
